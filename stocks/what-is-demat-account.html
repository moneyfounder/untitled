How the Demat Ecosystem Works

  • Depository: NSDL or CDSL
  • Depository Participant (DP): Authorized brokers or banks
  • Investor: You, the account holder

When you buy shares, your broker initiates the transaction, which is processed by the exchange and then the depository updates your Demat account. Selling shares works in reverse, all done electronically without paperwork.

Advanced Types of Demat Accounts

  • Repatriable Demat Account: For NRIs to invest and repatriate funds.
  • Non-Repatriable Demat Account: For NRIs without foreign remittance.
  • Corporate Demat Account: Used by companies.
