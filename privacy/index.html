Privacy Policy

Welcome to Money Founder. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit or use our website and services. By accessing or using Money Founder, you agree to the terms outlined here.

1. Information We Collect

Money Founder is primarily an educational platform and does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or payment information. The data we handle is limited to:

  • Non-Personal Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized information including page views, session durations, and general geographic regions (e.g., country or city, without specific user identification).
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, and IP addresses, collected solely for performance monitoring and user experience optimization.

All data is collected through trusted third-party services and is never linked to individual users.

2. Use of Analytics and Tracking Technologies

We employ third-party tools to understand how users interact with Money Founder to improve our services. These include:

  • Google Tag Manager (GTM): For managing analytics and marketing tags efficiently.
  • Google Analytics: To analyze anonymous traffic patterns and user engagement.

These services use cookies and similar technologies only to collect non-personal, aggregated data. We do not share or sell any data collected. For more information on their data practices, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies

By continuing to use this site, you consent to the use of cookies that help improve site functionality and analytics. While Money Founder itself does not store personal information via cookies, third-party tools may place cookies for purposes such as:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies used during your visit to analyze website usage.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Cookies set by providers like Google Analytics to track anonymized traffic and behavior.

You may manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Please note this may affect certain features or site performance.

4. No User Accounts or Personal Data Collection

Our platform does not require you to create an account or submit personal data to access educational content. We do not request or store contact information, payment details, or sensitive personal data.

5. Your Rights and Controls

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights under applicable privacy laws:

  • Right to Transparency: You may request information about the data collected and processed.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can disable cookies or opt out of analytics tracking via browser settings or tools such as the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: For push notifications, you may unsubscribe at any time through your device or browser settings.

6. Data Security

We take the security of all data seriously, even if limited to non-personal analytics. We use industry-standard measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-party service providers we rely on (such as Google) also maintain strong security controls consistent with global standards.

7. Compliance with Laws

Money Founder complies with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to:

  • The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area.
  • The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for users in California.
  • Relevant Indian privacy and IT laws, as Money Founder operates primarily under Indian jurisdiction.
  • Other applicable international privacy laws where our services are accessed.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or applicable age depending on your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us to request removal.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or technology.

We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
