Sustainable construction practices are no longer optional‚Äîthey're expected. Developers now prioritize energy efficiency, low-carbon materials, and green certifications to attract conscious consumers.

Post-pandemic trends continue to influence where people live. There's a noticeable shift from central urban areas to suburban and peri-urban locations offering more space and lower prices.

Luxury real estate in suburban pockets is booming, particularly in the U.S. Sunbelt (Florida, Texas, Arizona), which combines affordability with quality of life.

Commercial Real Estate: Adapting to Hybrid Work

Office spaces are being reimagined. Demand has shifted to coworking spaces and hybrid-friendly environments.

Data centers and last-mile logistics hubs are seeing rapid growth, driven by e-commerce and cloud computing.

Retail spaces are being redesigned into mixed-use developments that include residential, office, and entertainment areas.

Vacation and Second Homes

The rise in remote work has made vacation homes a viable second residence rather than occasional retreats. Coastal properties in Portugal, Spain, and the Caribbean are popular among North American and European investors alike.

United States
